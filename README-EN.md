## рҹҗҳ Day 1 Highlight: Elephant Mountain (Xiangshan) вҖ” Iconic Taipei 101 Photo

Your friend's recommendation is spot on! Elephant Mountain offers THE classic postcard view of Taipei 101. Only a 15-20 minute hike to the top вҖ” super easy!

- **рҹҡҮ Transport:** Take MRT Red Line (Tamsui-Xinyi Line) to **"Xiangshan Station" (иұЎеұұз«ҷ)**, Exit 2
- **рҹҡ¶ Walk:** Follow the path alongside the park ~650m (~20 min) to the trailhead
- **рҹҘҫ Hike:** ~15-20 min to the top, all stairs. **Turn LEFT at the fork!** After ~8-10 min, there's a landing area with restrooms where the path branches
- **рҹ“ё Best Time:** Late afternoon вҖ” catch sunset plus daytime and nighttime views of Taipei 101. The rock formations at the top are the classic photo spot
- **рҹ“Қ Nearby:** Elephant Mountain is right next to Taipei 101/Xinyi Shopping District вҖ” only a 15-min walk from the base to 101. Can combine with Taipei 101 Observatory visit
- **рҹҚң Nearby Night Market:** Tonghua Night Market (Linjiang Street) вҖ” 25-30 min walk from the base, or 2 MRT stops to Xinyi Anhe station. Or head back to the city for Ningxia Night Market

> **рҹ’Ў Tip:** You're arriving on a red-eye flight at 6:15 AM. Drop bags at the hotel, rest/sleep until 3-4 PM, then head to Elephant Mountain вҖ” perfect timing for sunset!

## рҹҡЈ Day 12 Highlight: Xiuguluan River Rafting (з§Җе§‘е·’жәӘжіӣиҲҹ)

Your friend's recommendation is spot on! Xiuguluan River rafting is THE most iconic outdoor activity in eastern Taiwan. September is prime rafting season вҖ” good water levels and stable weather.

- **рҹ“Қ Location:** Ruisui Township, Hualien County вҖ” Ruisui Bridge вҶ’ Changhong Bridge, ~24km total
- **вҸұ Duration:** ~3-4 hours (including prep time)
- **рҹ—“ Best Season:** MayвҖ“October; September has ideal water levels and stable weather
- **рҹҺ« Booking:** Book in advance on Klook / KKday вҖ” packages typically include: gear (life jacket + helmet), rubber raft + guide, shuttle from Ruisui Bridge start point to Changhong Bridge finish, insurance, shower facilities
- **рҹ’° Cost:** ~NT$800вҖ“1,200/person (depending on package)
- **рҹҺ’ Bring:** Clothes you can get wet (you WILL be soaked!), change of clothes, towel, sunscreen, water shoes (best)
- **вҷЁпёҸ After Rafting:** Strongly recommend soaking in Ruisui Hot Springs afterward! Famous sodium bicarbonate springs ("beauty springs") вҖ” rafting + hot springs is the locals' favorite combo

> **рҹ’Ў Tip:** Rafting usually starts 7-8 AM, so staying in Ruisui the night before (9/29) is the best setup. After rafting (~noon), take the TRA south to Kaohsiung (RuisuiвҶ’TaitungвҶ’Kaohsiung, ~4-5 hours). Arrive Kaohsiung evening for the night market. The long train ride is perfect for a post-rafting nap!
